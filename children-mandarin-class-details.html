Children Mandarin Class

Introduction

Our Children Mandarin class helps kids under 12 year old to develop their interest in learning Chinese, and improve their Chinese proficiency.

The students will strengthen their vocabulary and grammar knowledge through interactive teaching and classroom learning activities. We emphasize to educate through entertainment, letting children to learn Chinese in a relaxed and entertaining environment.

Our course aims to help the students to

  • learn Pinyin, vocabulary and grammar in a fun way
  • improve reading and writing skills
  • be better prepared for UPSR Mandarin exam
  • express ideas more clearly and confidently in Mandarin

The program is mainly for

  • children who are currently studying or preparing to study in Chinese primary school in Malaysia
  • Chinese or non-Chinese children who have not received any formal Chinese education

We use small class size within 6 students to give each student a full guidance from the teacher and participation in activities. This helps to enhance the comprehensive ability to practice listening, speaking, reading and writing.
